Unique Zones

Giant's Cave

In the Era of Myths, some of the Giants avoided the wrath of Einhasad's cold eyes by escaping to the lost High Plateau. With the passing of time, the giants gradually degenerated, until they found themselves controlled by the mechanical devices they had themselves created. Those unfortunate giants that were absorbed by the machines lost the dignity of a high race and descended to the status of pitiful evil creatures. Although these giants have deteriorated intellectualy, their large, strong bodies are still fearsome to the Humans.

Unique Zones

Monastery of Silence

The Monastery of Silence was established and built by the Order of Saint Solina, a branch of the Einhasad Temple. The place serves for intense prayers and meditation that require long-term seclusion from the outside world. Because of its cloistered location, it is free from wars and political conflicts. The priests still preserve the old tradition of the Order. There are rumours that incredible holy items are stored inside the chambers of the monastery.

God Bosses

Baylor

Orc Demon King “Baylor,” summoned by Beleth, placed under a powerful mind control. When Baylor's was cursed, Beleth turned the whole island into a prison by erecting a wall of tremendous magic around it.

God Bosses

Tauti

When Tauti received Shilen's command, he led his followers, to Giant's Cave in order to expand Shilen's influence on the Continent. Tauti is carrying out Shilen's command, but he does not hide the fact that he is also seeking to increase his own power.

God Bosses

Balok

With a body covered with horns and claws, born with the ambition to destroy the Isle of Prayer and lead the army from hell to invade the world. The demon Balok was created by the witchcraft of the magician Beleth. With the Velakia sword in hand, Balok is the only demon with the power to break through the gates of hell. However, Beleth could barely control the power of the bloodthirsty demon Balok. It broke all of Beleth's spells and escaped into the Pagans, turning it into a hell with no return.

God Bosses

Octavis

Octavis was once the most powerful warrior in the world. One who participated in the war against Einhasad. More than that, Octavis is also Spezion's best friend. But because of his passion for Einhasad's daughter, Angel Paulina, Octavis betrayed humanity. Octavis' betrayal led to the defeat of the war and sent Spezion to prison. As a reward, Einhasad conferred the title of hero and made Octavis the supreme god of the Orbis temple, the temple of heroes. However, Shilen's hatred awakened the forces of darkness to invade the temple and turn Octavis and his army into his effective minions. Make Orbis a forbidden place of doom, anyone who enters must die.

God Bosses

Istina

The Queen of Annihilation Istina continues to create new creatures and test their abilities. Her quest is to create the most powerful creatures of all that can threaten the Material Plane. The very heart of annihilation herself, she watches over everything that happens inside the Swampland from its deepest reaches.
